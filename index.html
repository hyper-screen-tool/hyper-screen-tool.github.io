      About the model

      This tool implements the 19-feature elastic-net penalized logistic regression model used for external validation. Predictors were selected by backward stepwise regression, then the model was trained on three derivation cohorts (CAFPINT, PALI, REDVENT) with α = 0.15 and positive-class weight = 5.5.

      Patients with predicted probability ≥ 0.70 are classified as hyperinflammatory; values below 0.70 are classified as hypoinflammatory.

      Model development & validation (R)

      Missing data

      Missing predictors are replaced with the median value from the combined development dataset, matching the imputation strategy used during model training. Imputed fields are listed in the results panel.

      Model coefficients are available in coefficients.json.

      HYPER-SCREEN is a research decision-support tool and is not a substitute for clinical judgment. Predictions should be interpreted in the context of the full clinical picture. For research use and screening enrichment only.
